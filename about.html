About Global Finance Insights

Your trusted source for international financial market analysis

Welcome to Global Finance Insights, a premier platform for comprehensive analysis of international financial markets and economic developments, created by a team of finance students from the University of Moratuwa.

This initiative was developed as part of our IM4261 International Financial Management course, where we combine academic theory with real-world financial analysis to deliver insightful weekly market commentary.

Our Mission:

  • Deliver data-driven analysis of global financial trends
  • Connect classroom concepts with current market realities
  • Enhance practical financial analysis skills
  • Provide accessible insights for students and professionals

All content is meticulously researched from authoritative sources including Reuters, IMF reports, and central bank publications, ensuring accurate and timely market perspectives.
