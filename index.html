India’s Robust GST Collections in August 2025: A Sign of Economic Resilience and Policy Impact

India witnessed an unprecedented rise in its Goods and Services Tax (GST) collections, which touched ₹1.86 trillion (around $21.26 billion) in August 2025, up 6.5% year-on-year.
